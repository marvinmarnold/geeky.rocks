Consulting

Can't figure out if you should outsource or go local? Need help auditing your contract programmer? We gotcha covered for just $100 a day.

Meet a geek
Marvin Arnold

Hi, I am Marvin and I started Geeky NOLA in 2015. I graduated from MIT in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science and recently earned my MBA from IE Business School as a Fulbright Scholar and member of the Beta Gamma Sigma Honors Society.

My main job is founder of a tech startup called Unplugged. Geeky NOLA is my way to give back to the city and help the community take advantage of technology to its fullest. For me, Geeky NOLA isn't about making money but helping out.

We got
The skills

Whether you have a personal blog or are working on the next Facebook, we got the skills to take you to the next step.

Our team has been fixing computers since we were kids. As adults, we've made cloud based website, mobile applications, and contributed to leading open source projects.

Some companies our team has worked for include Goldman Sachs, Booz Allen Hamilton, the Financial Regulatory Authority, the Mars Biogravity Satellite Project, the MIT Media Lab, DKT International, Population Services International and Middle East Education through Technology. We have a particular passion for projects around the globe including work in Haiti, Belgium, Jerusalem, Spain, Jamaica, Rwanda, Mozambique, Japan, and the USA.
